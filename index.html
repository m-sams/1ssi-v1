What is 100 Suits Student Interaction (1SSI)?

100 Suits Student Interaction (1SSI) is a branch of the original 100 Suits organization. 1SSI targets students from the Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) to encourage participation in 100 Suits organized events in Queens, Brooklyn, and Manhattan (i.e: food drive, PPE distribution, etc.). 1SSI also provides paid work experiences with the 100 Suits organization. All participants and employees will receive a clothespin and keychain.

Volunteer for Upcoming Events

Are you a BMCC student interested in volunteering at an upcoming event? Please stay updated on the “Our Work” section, follow 100 Suits on Instagram, or visit an office location and sign up.

Paid Experiences

Are you a BMCC student looking for interesting and socially-minded work? Apply for one of the positions below through 1SSI. Please email us your resume and tell us why you’re interested.

  • Events Coordination Team

    Help decide where it would be useful to organize 100 Suits promotion events, what partnerships with other businesses would be largely beneficial to the advancement of the organization, and participate in planned events to increase community outreach with the 100 Suits organization. This ranges from standing and speaking at 100 Suits panels and booths at select venues to handing out flyers and more.

  • Crisis Management (CMS)

    Respond to or prevent violent occurences including but not limited to arguments, fights, or shootings. You will be subject to special training to be eligible for this work under 100 Suits.

  • Donations Management

    Pick up, quality inspect, or sort clothing donations sent to 100 Suits offices.

  • Office Aide

    Answer phone calls and provide information about the organization and what qualifies for donations.

  • Maintenance Team

    Clean and organize 100 Suits office locations.
